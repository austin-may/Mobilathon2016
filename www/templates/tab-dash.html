Heroes On The Water

September 3, 2016

Mid-Atlanta Area

Join us to continue the fishing season with a great day on the water. Equipment, food and lots of laughs will be provided at no charge for active duty servicemembers, veterans and their families. We will be fishing at Randleman Lake. Restroom facilities will be available. Children's activities will be provided. A playground and picnic area with space to run will be available as well.
